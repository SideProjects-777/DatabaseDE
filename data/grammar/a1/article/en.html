Articles

In German there are definite, indefinite, middle, masculine and feminine, plural and null articles. You should not try to understand all the articles at once, as experienced teachers never try to burden the student with explaining all the rules at once. In this article we will look at the basic rules of article formation in German.
Articles by gender: der, die, das
It is important to remember that each German noun has its own specific gender. Therefore, it is necessary to memorize the words together with the corresponding articles by gender - der, das, die. It is good that all plural nouns have only one article - die.
To make learning German easier, there are several hints in the form of suffixes in the grammar, which help to determine the gender of a noun without the need for memorization.

der

Prefix Example
-er der Fehler, der Schuler
–or der Lektor
–ent der Student
–ant der Laborant
-ling der Schmetterling
-at der Diplomat
-ist der Kommunist

It should also be pointed that any time of day der Abend,any day of week der Montag, any month der Januar, any season der Sommer and cardinal directions der Norden
Weather: der Wind (besides das Wolke), alcoholic beverages (besides das Bier)

das

Prefix Example

Prefix Example
